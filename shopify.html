Where to host your website: Shopify

Choosing the website hosting service that is the right fit for you can be a hard process. In this article, we will walk you through an amazing option: Shopify.


There are many places to host your website, but which should you choose? Shopify is a great tool to help beginners build websites fast and reliably. Shopify, though not as historic as some of it's competitors, has quickly made a name for itself as an amazing choice for website hosting. That being said, all options have pros and cons:

Pros

  • Shopify works well for larger businesses through an advanced inventory management system, leading to less stress for business owners
  • Shopify offers many apps and extensions to customize your store to your personal likings
  • There is no prior coding experience required to build a great looking website

    • Cons

  • Additional feeson Shopify can make a visible impact on monthly spendings for your businesss
  • While no prior coding experience is required, Shopify may take you some time to "figure out"


    • Before you decide on a service to host your website on, it is important to research all of the options in order to choose what is right for your company.
