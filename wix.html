Where to host your website: Wix

Choosing the website hosting service that is the right fit for you can be a hard process. In this article, we will walk you through yet another great option: Wix.


Wix is the last of the "Big Three". Wix is known to the proffessionals as an extremely solid choice for hosting. Despite this, even they have pros and cons:

Pros

  • Wix offers great features to view edits to your website live as you make them
  • Using Wix gives a lower cost to get your website up and running
  • There is no prior coding experience required to build your website

    • Cons

  • Wix does not provide deep analytics for your website
  • It can be difficult to switch website templates after you have already published your site using one


    • Before you decide on a service to host your website on, it is important to research all of the options in order to choose what is right for your company.
